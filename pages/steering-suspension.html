Steering & Suspension

The chances are that your steering and suspension are the last things on your mind when you are driving your vehicle, but these systems are just as important as any other system in your car. Steering and suspension are what allow you to handle your vehicle and give you control when you are behind the wheel. When these systems start to malfunction it can affect other components of your vehicle as well as put you in a potentially dangerous situation. LP & Sons Auto Care wants to keep you in control of your vehicle and enjoying the ride with our steering and suspension repair services.

Steering and Suspension Parts

Every model does not necessarily have the same steering and suspension components, but these systems all include some of these parts:

  • Springs
  • Shock Absorbers
  • Ball Joints
  • Idler Arm
  • Pitman Arm
  • Center Link
  • Steering Gear Box
  • Struts
  • Tie Road End

Anytime one of these parts wears out, loosens, or becomes damaged it compromises your vehicles’ stability. We recommend that you have your steering and suspension components checked regularly for any wear or damage from the road.

Signs of Steering and Suspension Trouble

In between maintenance and inspection appointments, you may start to notice problems with your suspension. When that happens, get your vehicle to LP & Sons Auto Care as soon as you can so we can correct the issue. Some signs of suspension trouble include:

  • An increasingly bouncy ride when you go over a bump or come to a stop
  • Pulling to the left or right when you are in motion
  • Abnormal or uneven tire wear
  • Difficulty turning or steering
  • Vibrations coming from the steering wheel
