Why Are Oil Changes So Important?

There are several reasons why your oil changes are important. The main reason is that your engine parts require the oil to allow them to glide against each other and reduce friction. The other important reason is it pulls heat away from the engine. If your engine overheats it can lead to parts being broken and wearing down faster. Clean and fresh motor oil will prevent your engine from overheating by keeping it cool.

Your oil helps keep the engine clean by collecting the dust that is picked up off the road. It is important to regularly replace this engine oil before it gets too dirty to prevent clogging of the engine. As the oil passes through the engine, it will naturally pick up the dust particles and become sludge over time. An engine with clean engine oil is a fuel-efficient engine, meaning with regular oil changes your average fuel economy will increase.

Increase Your Engine Life

Picture in your mind a battery in a toy. The more that toy is played with it the sooner the battery will run out of juice. The same holds true with your vehicle’s engine. The more you drive your car the more wear the “battery” (engine oil) will receive. Replacing the engine oil is the same as replacing the batteries in that toy, it will keep the toy running long just like your engine will run longer.
